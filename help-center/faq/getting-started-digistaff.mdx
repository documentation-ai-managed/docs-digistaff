## Overview

To begin using DigiStaff AI change management services:

1. Schedule an onboarding call at `https://digistaff.ai/onboard`
2. Receive a detailed project roadmap from our team
3. Add your team leads to DigiStaff portal (invite link provided after onboarding)

## Next Steps

- Attend an AI fluency workshop (calendar invite sent after registration)
- Explore the DigiStaff portal for training and resources

For more info, contact support@digistaff.ai
