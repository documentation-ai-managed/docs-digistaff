## Overview

Book a group AI training session for your department in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to DigiStaff portal">
    Navigate to `https://portal.digistaff.ai` and login with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Training">
    Select the "AI Fluency Training" tab in the sidebar menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a slot">
    Click "Schedule" to pick a date and time that fits your team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite participants">
    Enter emails of all team members to send them calendar invites automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
