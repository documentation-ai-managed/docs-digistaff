---
title: Cannot Access AI Training Portal
description: Quick fix for DigiStaff training access issues.
---

## Symptoms

- Error on portal login
- Can't load course materials

## Causes

- User not invited
- Account activation incomplete

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check invitation email">
    Locate your DigiStaff invite in your inbox and click the activation link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    If activated but unable to log in, go to the portal login page and use "Forgot password".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If issues continue, email support@digistaff.ai for assistance.
  </Step>
</Steps>
